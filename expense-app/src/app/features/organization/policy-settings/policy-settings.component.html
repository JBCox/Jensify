help_outline How Policies Work

Policy Priority

When multiple policies apply to an expense, the most specific one wins:

  1. User-specific policies override everything
  2. Category-specific policies for the expense type
  3. Department policies for the user's department
  4. Role-based policies (employee, manager, etc.)
  5. Organization-wide policies as fallback

Within each scope level, higher priority numbers win.

Spending Limits

Configure maximum amounts per transaction, per day, or per month. Expenses exceeding limits will be flagged for review.

Approval Thresholds

Set auto-approval for small expenses and require additional approval for large ones.
